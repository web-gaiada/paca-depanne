Frequently Asked Questions

Find the answers to your questions about our plumbing services

What emergency plumbing services do you cover?

We cover major water leaks, burst pipes, water damage, and blocked drain problems requiring prompt intervention.

How can I detect a hidden water leak?

Signs include an unexplained increase in your water consumption, damp patches, mold, or a dripping sound in the walls. We use non-destructive methods (such as thermal imaging) to locate them.

Are you installing new sanitary equipment?

Yes, we carry out the complete installation of all your sanitary equipment: showers, bathtubs, sinks, toilets, and the complete renovation of bathrooms.
