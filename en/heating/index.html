Frequently Asked Questions

Find the answers to your questions about our air conditioning services

What is the average lifespan of an air conditioning system?

With regular maintenance, a properly installed air conditioning system by a professional can last between 10 and 15 years, or even longer.

Is annual maintenance mandatory for my air conditioning?

For appliances containing more than 2 kg of refrigerant (approximately 12 kW), annual maintenance is mandatory. In all cases, it is strongly recommended to guarantee performance and hygiene.

How long does it take to install a reversible air conditioner?

Installing a single-split unit typically takes one day. For a more complex multi-split system, it can take between two and three days.
